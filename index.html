About Us

Who we are and the insight about Super Cars: A super car is sometimes called an exotic car, is a $100,000-plus ultra-high-performance sports car or grand tourer. Examples include the Chevrolet Corvette, new Ford GT or Dodge Viper. Other super cars come from exclusive automakers like Ferrari, Lamborghini or Rolls-Royce. Either way, a supercar is almost always limited in production. Less than 5,000 are produced and sometimes the production runs are as low 100.
